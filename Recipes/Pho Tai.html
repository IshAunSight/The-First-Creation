What Is "Pho Tai" ?

"Pho Tai" Is A Vietnamese Noodle Soup Dish.

Which Usually Consists Of: Beef Broth "Pho" And Some Form Of Meat. This Dish Uses "Tai" "or Thinly Sliced Beef"
